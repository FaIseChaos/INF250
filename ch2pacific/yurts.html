Yurts at Pacific Trails

What is a yurt?
Our luxury yurts are permanent structures for feet off the ground. Each yurt has canvas walls, a wooden floor, and a roof dome that can be opened.
How are the yurts furnished?
Each yurt is furnished with a queen-size bed with down pillows and comforters, a small kitchenette, and a private bathroom with a shower. Your yurt includes heated towel racks and a hair dryer.
What should I bring?
Bring food to cook in the kitchenette or on your outdoor grill. Bring hiking boots to explore the nature trails, and a camera to capture the beautiful views of the Pacific Ocean.
